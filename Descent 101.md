##### TURN, PLAYER

* **Movement** - ALLTID INTERRUPTIBLE (kan t.ex move-search-move)
* **Rest** - Måste göras sist, återställ all Fatigue
* **Search** - Om adjacent eller på en Search Token
* **Stand Up** - ENDA ACTION TILLÅTEN. Rulla 2 x Röd, Heart = HP och Bolt = Fatigue
* **Revive Hero** - Om adjacent
* **Open/Close Door** - Om adjacent



Start/End of Turn struktur på kort



KOM IHÅG FATIGUE = MOVEMENT, NÄRSOMHELST



##### TURN, OVERLORD

1. Draw one card
2. Start of Turn (cards, quest?)
3. Monster Activation
4. End of Turn (cards, quest?)



##### PLAYER DEFEAT (KNOCKED OUT)

* Player's turn ends (if he dies during his turn)
* Replace figure with token
TOKEN DOES NOT BLOCK ANYBODY'S MOVEMENT -OR- PLACEMENT (figure re-enters in nearest empty space if "corpse space" is occupied)
* Receive maximum Damage and Fatigue
* Remove all Condition cards
* OVERLORD DRAWS A CARD



##### ENCOUNTER TRANSITION

* Players recover Fatigue
* Players recover Heroic Feat
* Knocked out players Stand Up



##### CAMPAIGN PHASE (BETWEEN QUESTS)

1. Guld från Search kort
2. Nollställ alla spelare
3. Receive Rewards: +1XP till alla + ytterligare rewards från Quest Guide
4. Shop
Köpa: slumpa 5 kort
Sälja: halva värdet, runda till multipel av 5 (starting equipment alltid 25)
Relics kan inte säljas. Starting equipment hamnar inte i shoppen. Resten går tillbaka till shoppen.
Trading: fri trade, förutom Starting equipment
5. Alla spelare kan köpa skills (kom ihåg school-based gating för Overlord, 1-1-2-3)
6. Setup Quest
7. Travel



##### SETUP, OVERLORD

* FÅR discarda ner till 15
* Draw cards equal to players



##### FAMILIARS

Active once per turn - no action cost - but before OR after player actions

Får alltid göra en Movement Action + de actions som listas på kortet



FAMILIARS ÄR INTE FIGURES (om det inte står på kortet)

* Tar inte upp plats för movement/placement, blockar inte LOS
* During Movement, treat all terrain as Water (except Obstacles)
* Cannot be targeted by attacks



Om Figure: standard Hero beteende



##### TERRAIN

* **Röd Obstacle**
Blocked Space (blockar Movement och LOS)
* **Blå Water**
Kostar 2 MP att gå in i (stora monster kan "spilla över" efter movement)
* **Grön	Pit**
Kostar 2 HP att FALLA IN i
Kan inte spendera Movement Points
Action: Climb into adjacent space
Kan bara se, och ses av, figurer i adjacent rutor
Big monsters faller bara i Pit om de bara ockuperar Pit spaces
* **Yellow Lava**
Kostar 1 HP att gå in i
Om avslutar tur i Lava: Spelare defeated, Monster dör
Big monsters dör bara av Lava om de enbart ockuperar Lava space



##### TRADING ITEMS

Can be done freely during a MOVE action

New equipment is NOT immediately equipped

Starting equipment can NOT be traded

##### RULE HIERARCHY

Scenario Book > Cards > Rule book



##### STATUS EFFECT APPLICATION

If Attack: Must deal at least 1 damage

If not attack: No requirement



##### LINE OF SIGHT

Figurer blockerar LOS (både vänliga och fientliga)

Obstacles blocker LOS



##### ATTACK RANGE

Vid beräkning av range ignoreras figurer och obstacles, skottet går rakt igenom (LOS måste förstås finnas)



##### HEALING A KNOCKED OUT HERO

He recovers that much Damage and Figure is placed on board (no Stand Up dice rolling)



##### BIG MONSTER MOVEMENT

They "shrink" while moving

Only enter a Pit if their movemens ends or is interrupted so that each space they occupy is a pit space



##### TILES

Blocked Space		Contains Figure or Obstacle

Empty Space		No Figures, doesn't block movement or LOS



##### RACE CONDITION ON EFFECTS (BETWEEN PLAYERS)

Current player determines order



##### EQUIPMENT LIMITS

2 Hand, 1 Armor, 2 Other

Ingen gräns på items utan slot indicator

Can be opened and closed, by both heroes and monsters
